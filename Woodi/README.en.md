# Woodi – the playful Codex otter

<img align="left" width="220" src="preview-transparent.png" alt="Woodi, an otter with a wooden stick">

[Deutsche Version](README.md)

Woodi is a friendly, curious otter who plays with a small wooden stick while helping Codex. He waves, moves, jumps, waits for input, works with focus, reviews results, and looks around in every direction.
