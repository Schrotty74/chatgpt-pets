# WonderCube – the thoughtful puzzle cube

<img align="left" width="220" src="preview-transparent.png" alt="WonderCube, a friendly puzzle cube">

[Deutsche Version](README.md)

WonderCube is a friendly puzzle cube who visibly solves itself one move at a time while working. He thinks through the next turn while waiting, gets briefly scrambled after a setback, and proudly presents a solved face at the end.
